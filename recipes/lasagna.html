Lasagna

Lasagna dish with beef meat and wheat

Simple and delicious dish

Ingredients:

  • Wheat
  • Beef meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Cheese
  • Spices and seasoning

Steps to make it:

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest
