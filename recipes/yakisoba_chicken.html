Yakisoba Chicken

Exotic chicken noodle from Asia

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons chili paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 medium head cabbage
  • 2 carrots
  • 8 ounces soba noodles

Steps to make it:

  1. Combine sesame and canola oil, chili paste
  2. Stir and garlic.
  3. Fry everything
  4. Add cabbage, chicken and carrots
